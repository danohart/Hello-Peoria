## Hello Peoria

This website serves as a way to learn what to do in Peoria, IL. Choose a path like free things to see and do, to foodie path for the best restuarants and tips on local fare. Or family friendly activities, to night life spots for a late night out dancing and drinking.

### Create your own list

Click "Add to list" on multiple places and then click "Save list" to create your own sharable list for out of town guests, or just your own favorites list for friends and family.

### Purpose

The purpose of this site is to not only inform, but to help others think more local but supporting these businesses that are locally owned and have a local impact on their community. What we do not have is large chain spots.
